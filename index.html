About Me

I work on quantum many-body physics, with a focus on dipolar Bose–Einstein condensates, quantum simulation, and computational approaches to emergent quantum phenomena. My research explores supersolidity, droplet formation, and non-equilibrium dynamics in interacting quantum systems.
